## Requesting a Part

### Step 1: Tap "Request a Part"

From the home screen, tap the **"Request a Part"** button (the large green button with the + icon).

### Step 2: Enter Vehicle Details

1. **Vehicle Make:** Select from the dropdown (e.g., Toyota, BMW, VW)
2. **Vehicle Model:** Select the model (e.g., Corolla, 3 Series, Golf)
3. **Vehicle Year:** Enter the year (e.g., 2018)

### Step 3: Describe the Part

1. **Part Name:** Enter what you're looking for (e.g., "Front brake pads")
2. **Part Category:** Select the category (Engine, Brakes, Suspension, etc.)
3. **Description:** Add any specific details:
   - OEM part number (if known)
   - New vs. used preference
   - Any specific brand requirements

### Step 4: Add Photos (Recommended)

1. Tap **"Add Photo"**
2. Take a photo of the broken part or the part location
3. Good photos help shops identify the correct part faster!

### Step 5: Select Your Location

1. Tap **"Set Location"**
2. Allow location access OR manually enter your suburb
3. Shops within your area will see your request

### Step 6: Submit Request

1. Review your request details
2. Tap **"Submit Request"**
3. Your request will be sent to nearby shops automatically

# ğŸ”§ TROUBLESHOOTING

## Common Issues

### "Login Failed"

**Cause:** Incorrect email or password  
**Solution:**
1. Check your email spelling
2. Use "Forgot Password" to reset
3. Check if Caps Lock is on

### "Unable to Submit Request"

**Cause:** Missing required fields  
**Solution:**
1. Ensure all fields are filled
2. Check internet connection
3. Try again in a few seconds

### "Quote Expired"

**Cause:** You took too long to accept  
**Solution:**
1. Quotes expire after 48 hours
2. Request a new quote from the shop
3. Or submit a new part request

### "Payment Failed"

**Cause:** Card declined or network issue  
**Solution:**
1. Check card details
2. Ensure sufficient funds
3. Try a different payment method
4. Contact your bank if issues persist

### "Not Receiving Notifications"

**Cause:** Notifications disabled  
**Solution:**
1. Check phone settings â†’ SpareLink â†’ Notifications â†’ Enable
2. Check in-app settings â†’ Notifications â†’ Enable
3. Ensure Do Not Disturb is off
